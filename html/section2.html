About DataBlocks

DataBlocks was founded with a mission to democratize access to AI technologies for small and medium enterprises and local government authorities. We believe that these organizations have unique needs and challenges when it comes to AI adoption, and our goal is to provide tailored support and resources.

Our team consists of AI specialists, change management experts, and industry professionals who understand the specific contexts of SMEs and local governments. We work collaboratively with organizations to build their AI capabilities step by step, ensuring sustainable and responsible implementation.

As a charity, we are committed to making AI accessible to all organizations, regardless of their size or resources. We offer tiered services to meet different needs and budgets, with some resources available free of charge.

Notes on Implementation Services Grid: The services section uses a responsive grid layout that displays 3 items per row on desktop, 2 on tablet, and 1 on mobile. Service Cards: Each card follows a consistent pattern with a title, description, and action button. The blue accent line on the left side provides visual interest. About Layout: The about section uses a two-column layout on desktop and tablet that switches to a single column on mobile. Decorative Elements: The about-box contains geometric shapes that reinforce the Bauhaus-inspired design language of the site. Animation Hooks: Two highlight-row elements are included - one at the end of the services section and one at the end of the about section. The animation system will use these as reference points for the highlight squares. Container Usage: Both sections use the shared container class for consistent margins and maximum width. Semantic Structure: The HTML uses semantic markup with appropriate heading levels to maintain accessibility and SEO best practices.
